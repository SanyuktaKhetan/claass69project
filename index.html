Campus Life

SMART CLASSROOMS

Keeping space with the technology.

In view of keeping space with the technology, the smart boards and cc cameras are installed in all the classrooms. The classroom environment has become more interactive and interesting.





HOBBY CLASSES

Gurukul has included hobby classes in the regular time table

Gurukul has included hobby classes in the regular time table to help students discover their inherent talents and creativity. These include music – both vocal and instrumental - Bharatnatyam, Karate, art and craft, sports like football, basketball, volleyball, table tennis and badminton. These classes are conducted by eminent teachers and imbibe in the students a sense of discipline and aesthetics.





LIBRARY

A spacious and furnished library

A spacious and furnished library with a capacity of 60 seats is set, up in the senior secondary building to encourage reading among students. It is equipped with a good number of books for the students as well as reference books for the teachers Students are allotted library periods in their time table, which gives them an opportunity to read extensively.

SPORTS
Sports and games are integral part of our curriculum.

Sports and games are integral part of our curriculum. Students are provided ample opportunities to excel in their field. Every year more than 150 students represent Valsad district to participate at the State level tournaments.

CO – CURRICULAR
Besides the regular curriculum, a lot of importance is given to co-curricular and extra-curricular activities.

The students are exposed to various inter school. competitions in the district, including debates, quiz contests, synopsis and presentation etc.IKEN Scientific, India’s first Science and Technology Competition was conducted by Maa Foundation in Gurukul. After the Zonal level round held in Gurukul, six students were selected for the National Round at IIT Mumbai where Miss Poonam Yadav of class V was awarded the year 2009 IKEN student.
